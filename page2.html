A planetary system 40 light-years away with potential for habitability.

TRAPPIST-1 System

The TRAPPIST-1 system, located 40 light-years away, features seven Earth-sized planets that have the potential to support liquid water.

In March 2022, evidence of an atmosphere was found on one of its planets. The system has been studied with the Hubble Space Telescope and NASA's James Webb Space Telescope.

Discovery and Exploration

The TRAPPIST-1 system has garnered significant attention since its discovery due to its potential for hosting life. Research continues to explore the atmospheres of its planets to assess habitability.
